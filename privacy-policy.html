My Allergy Buddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information is collected, how it is used, and what rights you have when using this app.

2. What We Collect

a. User-Provided Information

  • Emergency contact names and phone numbers
  • Your phone number (for optional passcode or recovery features)
  • Allergy selections and medical notes you choose to enter
  • Personal profile details (e.g. name, phone number)

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device ID, crash logs, and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics
  • App usage statistics and user behaviour (via Firebase Analytics)
  • In-app event data (e.g. scans completed, premium unlocks)
  • Subscription status (via RevenueCat and Google Play)
  • GPS location when emergency features are manually activated

c. Security Information

  • Encrypted passcodes using AES-256
  • Secure storage using device keychain
  • Biometric data (if enabled) stored only on your device

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Power core features like allergen scanning, saving allergies, and sending emergency alerts
  • Monitor app performance and crash reporting
  • Log custom app usage events to improve functionality (e.g., feature popularity)
  • Track premium subscription status
  • Ensure secure access through passcodes or biometrics

4. Data Security

  • AES-256 encryption for local data
  • Secure device keychain for sensitive information
  • Firebase for secure backend logging
  • No health data is stored externally

5. Location Services

  • Location is only used during emergency contact alerts or calls
  • Shared only with your selected contacts
  • Never accessed in the background
  • Can be disabled via your device settings

6. SMS and Emergency Features

  • SMS is used to contact trusted persons during emergencies
  • Messages include your live GPS location (if permission is granted)
  • The emergency call feature dials 000 (Australia only)
  • You're responsible for keeping emergency info up to date
  • Standard SMS rates may apply

7. Data Storage

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is uploaded to external servers or cloud storage
  • Optionally backed up by your deviceâ€™s OS (if enabled)

8. Your Rights

  • Access or delete your data at any time
  • Disable location, biometrics, or permissions
  • Remove emergency contacts or passcodes
  • Fully erase all data by uninstalling the app

9. Data Retention

  • Data is retained only while the app is installed
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored information
  • We do not retain any backups outside your device

10. Third-Party Services

This app integrates with:

  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics
  • RevenueCat (to manage subscriptions)
  • Google Play Billing (for in-app purchases)
  • Google Maps (for emergency SMS links)
  • Native device APIs (camera, SMS, GPS)

These services follow their own privacy and security practices.

11. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features evolve. You'll be notified in-app of any significant changes.

13. Legal Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • No international data transfers occur

14. Emergency Data Use

  • Your location and info may be shared with trusted contacts
  • Based on public interest and safety
  • No emergency data is stored afterward

15. Breach Notification Procedure

  • You will be notified within 72 hours
  • Firebase will assist in diagnostics
  • Relevant privacy regulators will be contacted

16. No Automated Decision-Making

This app does not use profiling or AI-driven decisions. All features are user-controlled.

17. Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw consent by:

  • Disabling permissions in your device settings
  • Deleting emergency contacts
  • Uninstalling the app
